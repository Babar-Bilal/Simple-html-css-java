Markhor

Markhor is a combination of two Persian and Pashto worlds:

  • 'Mar' means Snake.
  • 'Khor' means Eater.
  • Snake Eater or Killer.

The markhor (Capra falconeri) /ˈmɑːrkɔːr/ is a large Capra species native to Central Asia, the Karakoram, and the Himalayas.It is listed on the IUCN Red List as Near Threatened since 2015. The markhor is the national animal of Pakistan, where it is also known as the screw horn or "screw-horned goat", mārkhor (مارخور) in Urdu from Classical Persian.
